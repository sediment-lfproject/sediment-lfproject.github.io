Maintaining

Process, Roles, Responsibilities and Guidelines

Committers

Committers have push access to the main branch of the sediment-lfproject github. Committers are generally in charge of merging pull requests from contributers and more or less have the final say in what gets merged into the main branch.

Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for which features get included and excluded from releases as well as being ultimately responsible for the releases themselves.
